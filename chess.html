Principles:

  • You do not move the same piece twice
  • You do NOT move the same piece twice
  • Control the center
  • Capture towards the center
  • Lose the Queen, lose the game
  • Castle ASAP (king side is faster)

Cool Openings:

  • Icelandic Gambit (black answer to 1. e4)
  • Danish Gambit (white sacky opening for fast development of the bishops)
